Short Biography

Hello, my name is Erica Ramirez and I was a fourth year Public Health Policy major with a minor in Health Informatics. A fun fact I always like to share is how I am allergic to cats, however, given my little sister is the favorite child, I am fortunate enough to have not just one cat in the house but 3!
UCI was always my dream school growing up. In elementary, every Friday a class would present a University or College. In 3rd grade, my class was given UCI to present. I was amazed by how beautiful it looked I would always say "When I'm older I want to go to UCI" and here I am years later on my fourth year.
